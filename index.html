About me

I have a PhD in computer science specialized in Human Computer Interaction (HCI)/ User Experience (UX) in health and road safety research settings.

Currently, I am working as a research fellow at RECOVER Injury Research Centre, at the University of Queensland working on design, development & evaluation of innovative technology solutions (i.e. virtual reality, digital health applications) to improve the delivery and availability of evidence-based health services.

I have a special interest in multidisciplinary research in human-centred design and designing technologies that are both useful and accepted by end-users. I am also passionate about advocating science, technology, engineering and mathematics (STEM) education among women.
